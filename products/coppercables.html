IKC-UTP-C5e - UTP Cable - Cat 5e - 24AWG

The cable features a 24AWG solid bare copper conductor with polyolefin (PO) insulation for high transmission performance and safety. It supports structured cabling for horizontal and backbone applications, transmitting digital and analog data, video, and audio. Compatible with IEEE 802.3u, CDDI, ATM, Token Ring, and PoE standards. The cable offers high transmission performance with a sweep frequency.
