FIELD TERMINATION PLUG - CAT.8.1/CLASS I

ikCom plug is made of fully sturdy metal housing. The plug is designed to be simple and easy to be terminated with the twisted cables at field. The IDC is designed to accommodate different diameter conductors up to 22AWG. All eight conductors are terminated at same time when the wire cap is squeezed by plier. This process ensures consistent, reliable termination.
